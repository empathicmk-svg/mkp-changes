**— Make real vocabulary decisions.** Build the word lists that do the quiet heavy lifting: the terms you own, the words you ban, the phrasings that signal "us" and the ones that signal "everyone else."

**— Document it for a team.** Turn all of it into a clean, shareable guide a new hire or freelancer can follow on day one, without a meeting.

This isn't a theory book about why brand voice matters. You already know it matters. It's a **build book** — structured, example-driven, and ruthlessly practical. Every chapter moves your actual voice document forward, with worked examples showing the method in action so you're never staring at a blank page.

- **In-house teams** scaling content across more people, channels, and freelancers

By the end, you won't have adjectives. You'll have a document — voice foundations, a tone map, vocabulary lists, do/don't examples, and team-ready guidelines — that makes everyone who writes for your brand sound like *you*.

- **No content warnings required.** Business/marketing nonfiction with no sensitive content.
- **No claims/guarantees:** Avoid outcome promises ("guaranteed conversions," "double your sales") in metadata — Amazon flags unverifiable claims and they undercut the premium positioning. Current copy stays in capability language ("learn how to," "build a"), which is compliant.
