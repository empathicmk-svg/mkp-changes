## Full sales description

Most businesses aren't unclear because they have nothing to say. They're unclear because they're saying it the way they said it three pivots ago — and patching new lines onto old bones.

The Rewrite tears that down and rebuilds it. This is my most requested service for a reason: it's the one that changes how people see you. We start with strategy, not sentences. What do you actually do that no one else does? Who is it for — really, not aspirationally? What are you being measured against in their head, and how do you win that comparison? Once that's locked, I rewrite the copy that carries it.

This isn't a polish pass. It's a positioning overhaul that shows up as words. The homepage that finally leads with the right claim. The about page that earns trust instead of asking for it. The offer descriptions that make the price feel obvious. The lines you'll repeat in every meeting because they're finally true and finally sharp.

You've been misread because your copy has been doing a worse job than your work deserves. People underestimate you on the strength of a paragraph. The Rewrite fixes the paragraph — and the underestimation goes with it.

I work in your voice, dialed up. Direct where you were soft. Specific where you were safe. Decisive where you were covering every base and landing nowhere. When it's done, you won't sound like a copywriter wrote it. You'll sound like the version of you that finally figured out how to say it.
