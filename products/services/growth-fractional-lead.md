## Full sales description

You don't need another consultant who hands you a strategy and disappears. You need someone who picks up the number and carries it.

There's a gap most growing companies hit. You're past the point where the founder can own growth on the side, but you're not ready — or not sure — to hire a full-time VP at full-time cost. So growth sits in limbo: half-owned, under-resourced, everyone's job and therefore no one's. The number drifts. Nobody's accountable. Meetings about it end in shrugs.

The Fractional Growth Lead closes that gap. I embed in your team as a senior operator who owns growth end to end — the strategy, the execution, the team, and most importantly the number. Not advising from the outside. Inside. In your standups, your tools, your roadmap. Accountable to the same metric you are, reporting like a leader on staff, not a vendor sending invoices.

This is ownership, not input. I set the growth strategy, prioritize the work, run or build the team that executes it, and stand behind the result. When the number moves, we know why. When it doesn't, I'm the one explaining it and fixing it — not the one pointing at someone else.

Because every company's gap is different — different stage, different team, different number — this engagement is scoped, not boxed. This listing is the paid scoping deposit. It secures a working scoping engagement where we define the number you need owned, the scope of the role, the cadence, and the terms. The deposit is fully credited toward your retainer once we begin. You're not buying a guess. You're buying a serious conversation that ends in a real plan.

You've been carrying growth in the margins of everyone's job. Hand it to someone whose entire job is to own it.
